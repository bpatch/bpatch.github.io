  Biography

From 2019 I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Stochastics group at CWI, the Dutch Center for Mathematics and Computer Science, as part of the NWO-VICI project Rare Events: asymptotics, algorithms, applications led by Bert Zwart. This project is at the interface of applied probability and power systems.

In 2019 I obtained a PhD (available here and here) in Mathematics jointly between University of Queensland and University of Amsterdam on Modelling Complex Stochastic Systems: Approaches to Management and Stability.
